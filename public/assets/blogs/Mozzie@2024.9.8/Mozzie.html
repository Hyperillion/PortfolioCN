Get Away Mozzie | 蚊子快跑

How to Play

Step 1: Find a place that is bright enough with a clear background.
Step 2: Click the "START/STOP" button to start the game.
OR Play in Full Screen
Step 3: Use your LEFT hand to catch all the mosquitoes.
Step 4: Enjoy! (Though probably not, especially with the sound on) :P

Useful Hints

1. A plain-colored background improves hand recognition.
2. Try to use the center of your palm to catch the mosquitoes.
3. If you miss a mosquito, it will escape.
4. A good strategy is to keep your hand still and wait the mosquitoes to come.
5. Annoyed by the sound? You can turn it off in the Control Panel.

Idea

The idea of this project is simple: kill all the mosquitoes.
